Services & Specialties

Trico owns 6 fully stocked tool trailers along with an extensive stock of specialty equipment.

  • We have automatic overlay equipment that we use on small overlay jobs
  • We also have our own heating equipment for preheating weld joints
  • Merit shop servicing mills around the country
  • Specializing in Kamyr digesters and associated equipment
  • We maintain NBIC “R” Stamp, ASME “S” and ASME “U” stamps, which allows us to perform Code repairs to pressure vessels and fabricate code items.
  • Moveable platforms for all sizes of continuous digesters
  • Umbrella scaffolding for batch digester work
  • Our QC Manager is Harland Webber. He has been with Trico for 30 years and was a former QC Manager with GE.
  • We do Code Calculations in house which allows us to keep the cost down and respond quickly.
  • Trico maintains numerous Digester climber scaffolds which can accommodate vessels from 10’ I.D. to 26’ I.D. and at any height.
  • Trico owns, inspects and maintains a stock of 26 Skyclimber motors which allow us to better respond to emergency situations.
  • Ability to install multiple skyclimber platforms to accommodate work from multiple levels at the same time.
